How to use?
* Choose Filters and click "Load" to load or update items from the official market, sorted by the profitability (earn minus cost).
* Add Auth Token from the market and connect your wallet for advanced features.
* Click the "Check" button to check if a lot is purchasable (if not, you can click the "Ignore" button to ignore that lot in future searches).
* Click on the "BUY" button and confirm the transaction in the Metamask to buy a hero (it will appear in your market inventory in a few minutes).
* You can use the tool without an Auth Token or Wallet connection (just use Market link).

How to get the Auth token to check lots?
* Go to the official market.
* Go to your "inventory", click F12, in the "Console" tab paste localStorage['theta/accessToken'] and click [Enter].
* Copy the long code and paste it into the "Market Auth token" field above.
* You will be able to check and BUY heroes right from this page (with out Token impossible to get data neede to send a transaction).

Website Features
* Easily check your WBNB balance, exchange rates and win rate.
* Easily convert between BNB and WBNB.
* Load items from the official market and sort by profitability.
* It is possible to quick sort by any collumn. * It is possible to check if an item can be bought and ignore faulty items.
* It is possible to buy heroes directly from this page.
* Profitability is calculated based on remaining fights, hero rarity, hero level and WinRate set.
* You can load lots using filters or most recently added lots.
* Set the minimum number of games remaining/percent games left and pages to load to optimize loading speed.
* Automatic refresh function with new items highlight.
